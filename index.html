About

Founded with a stable foundation and a passion for innovation, Sylvi Publishing is poised to make a lasting global impact. Our mission is to make learning seamless and enjoyable while fostering creativity. We are committed to publishing quality works that touch hearts—be it through evocative fiction, insightful nonfiction, or innovative digital formats—while nurturing your skills to reaching out to any corner of the world, literally and verbally.

Our Method

esque-learning™

Our esque-learning™ method makes learning foreign languages easier than ever by offering dual-language translated books, allowing stories to flow seamlessly between cultures and languages, fostering understanding and connection.
